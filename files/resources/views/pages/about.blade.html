About

Design is a craft. We treat it like one.

We've spent thirteen years designing for startups — first inside them, now alongside them. In that time we've learned that great design isn't about taste alone; it's about momentum. Founders don't need a hundred options, they need the right one, shipped.

Amber is deliberately small. Four people, no account managers, no hand-offs, no telephone game. You talk to the people doing the work, and the work moves fast because of it.
