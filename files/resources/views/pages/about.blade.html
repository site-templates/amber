About

Design is a craft. I treat it like one.

I've spent twelve years designing for startups — first inside them, now alongside them. In that time I've learned that great design isn't about taste alone; it's about momentum. Founders don't need a hundred options, they need the right one, shipped.

Calloway Studio is deliberately small. No account managers, no hand-offs, no telephone game. You talk to the person doing the work, and the work moves fast because of it.

When I'm not designing, I'm restoring a 1970s sailboat and writing an occasional letter about design and independence.
